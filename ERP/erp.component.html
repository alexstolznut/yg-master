Returns & Exchanges

We want you to be satisfied with your purchase, however if you need to return or exchange a product , you must do so within 30 days of the order date.

In order to keep our prices as low as possible, there are a couple of rules to follow:

  • Loose tea and herbal products must be unopened and include a copy of your invoice or packing slip with your order number. If any portion of the product is used, we cannot offer you an exchange or refund . After we received the returned product, we will refund you the credit.
  • For all other items, they must be in a new or unused condition with the original packaging present.

Our digital images are as accurate as possible. However, different monitors may cause colors to vary slightly.
