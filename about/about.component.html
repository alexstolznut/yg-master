About Us

Established in 2014 in Sacramento, California. Yongtai Ginseng has become the premier and trusted retailer of Asian products in Northern California。 Born from generations of herbal and tea wisdom, we take pride in offering the best in Chinese herbs, premium loose teas, ginseng, Asian delicacies, housewares, and fine wine and spirits.

永泰参茸公司於2014年加州沙卡缅度，至今成為北加州地區較具規模的亞洲商品誠信零售商。傳承世代經營藥材及茶葉的精髓，永合豐經營的貨品琳瑯滿目，品種繁千，包括有中藥材、雀石茗茶、花旗人參、亞洲美食、家用電器、世界名酒等。
