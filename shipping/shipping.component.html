Shipping Policy

Packaging of Loose Product

One quantity of loose leaf tea on our website is equivalent to 4 oz. (1/4 lb.) of tea. Loose leaf tea will come in a ziplock bag for easy storage. Every tea has a different density; some teas may take up to 2 bags because they are light and fluffy, while others make only take up only half a bag because they’re are compact and dense. If you order 1 pound of the loose leaf teas, you will receive two 8 oz. bags of tea or one 1 lb. bag of tea, depending on the size of the tea leaves. If you really want your order to be separated into 4 oz. bags, please contact us at yongtaiginseng@gmail.com. Please have your order number ready and let us know what you'd like to do. We are also able to do the same for ginseng and delicacies.

Destinations

Yongtai Ginseng ships to all 50 states and US Territories including American Samoa, Puerto Rico, Guam, and the US Virgin Islands. We do not ship to APO/FPO addresses or P.O. Boxes.

The orders for out of US, the buyer need pay the customs duties by yourself.

Order Tracking

o track your package, please go to the UPS/USPS website, and enter the tracking number sent to you in your order confirmation e-mail. If you do not see the e-mail, please check your Spam folder as it may have ended up there. You can also contact us atyongtaiginseng@gmail.com.

Shipping Time

If your order is to be shipped within Califrornia, your order will reach you in 3 to 5 days from the time it leaves our warehouse in Los Angeles, Ca. For all other states, it can take 5 to 10 days. Please be aware that the shipping companies who serve Yongtai Ginseng do not deliver products on weekends or holidays. Shipping charges are based on the weight of the item(s) you purchased and the level of service you select. Yongtai Ginseng only ships products to addresses in the United States. Please note that customers pay for the cost of shipping on returned merchandise.

Yongtai Ginseng is not responsible for any delays or errors made by carriers once the package has left our hands.

Shipping Alternatives

Domestically, we ship via USPS /UPS Mail, depending on the package weight and the region in which you live. Choosing a faster method of shipping does not decrease the processing time of an order, it only decreases the time it takes to get delivered.

Yongtai Ginseng accepts orders online 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. However, Friday and weekend orders are processed and prepared for shipping beginning the next business day. Therefore, these days are not included in the time needed to process your order. Most orders will be shipped within 3 business days if all the items ordered is in stock. If an item is out of stock, we will notify you by e-mail or by phone. You can choose another equal price item for instead. Or, you can choose to receive a refund in which we will refund you after the confirmation of your payment. Once we have issued a refund, we will notify you again by e-mail or by phone.

永泰可以郵寄商品到全美50個州以及美國薩摩亞群島，波多黎各，關島和維爾京群島；可是我們不郵寄到APO/FPO或P.O. Boxes。国际订单如遇关税及报关问题，由收件人自行负责。

郵寄方案

美國本土訂單，我們將通過UPS / USPS 郵寄貨品，運費取決於包裹的重量以及您居住的地區。選擇更快的郵寄方式不代表減少訂單處理的時間。它只是減少包裹的遞送時間。

永泰接受全天24小時，每週7天的網上訂單。但是，週五及週末的訂單將於下一個營業日開始處理和付運。所以，這些時間不被計入處理您的訂單所需的時間。大部分訂單在有庫存的情況下會在3個工作天內付運。若訂單內有某一貨品暫缺貨，我們會以電子郵件或電話聯繫的方式通知您。您可選擇另一等價貨品代替。也可選擇退款，我們將會在收到您的付款確認後兩周內處理退款。退款被處理後，我們會以電子郵件或電話聯繫的方式通知您。
